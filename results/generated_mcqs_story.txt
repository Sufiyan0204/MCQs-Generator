#1. Question: What led Zayd ibn Faris to the desert?
A) His desire to become a wealthy merchant
B) His father's exile after being falsely accused of treason
C) His fascination with desert wildlife
D) His quest to find a legendary oasis
Correct Answer: B

#2. Question: What artifact was said to be hidden within the lost city of Ubar?
A) The Staff of Thoth
B) The Jewel of the Sun
C) The Ring of Solomon
D) The Sword of Osiris
Correct Answer: B

#3. Question: What was the final trial Zayd faced in Ubar?
A) Trial of Strength
B) Trial of Wisdom
C) Trial of Honor
D) Trial of Courage
Correct Answer: C

#4. Question:  What happened to the Jewel of the Sun at the end of the story?
A) Zayd took it and used its power to rebuild Ubar.
B) Rafiq stole the jewel while Zayd was distracted.
C) The jewel vanished, its power returning to the heavens.
D) The djinn kept the jewel and continued guarding Ubar.
Correct Answer: C
